About ECP

OverView of ECP

For the purpose of election to both Houses of Majlis-e- Shoora (Parliament), Provincial Assemblies and for election to such other public offices as may be specified by law, a permanent Election Commission shall be constituted in accordance with this Article. The Election Commission shall consist of:

(a) the Commissioner who shall be Chairman of the Commission; and

(b) four members, one from each Province, each of whom shall be a person who has been a judge of a High Court or has been a senior civil servant or is a technocrat and is not more than sixty-five years of age, to be appointed by the President in the manner provided for appointment of the Commissioner in clauses (2A) and (2B) of Article 213.

Honourable CES

Mr. M. J. Sikandar Sultan Raja belongs to Bhera, district Sargodha. He completed his early education from the most prestigious institutions of the country. He did his Matric and F.Sc. from Cadet College Hassan Abdal and then graduated from King Edward Medical College Lahore with a degree in medicine. He also got degree in Law (LLB) from Punjab University. He got inducted into the civil service (DMG/PAS) through FPSC in 1987 and belongs to the 15th Common Training Program (15th CTP) Batch. After completing training, he was posted as Assistant Commissioner, Islamabad in 1989. He served on many key positions such as Deputy Commissioner, Islamabad, Director General Excise and Taxation Punjab, Secretary of Communication & Works, Services and General Administration, and Local Government and Community Development Departments in Punjab.
